Privacy Policy

App: KnowScore  ·  Last updated: 8 June 2025  ·  Developer: Sergei Soldatov  ·  Portugal, EU

This Privacy Policy explains how KnowScore ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application on iOS and Android. By using KnowScore you agree to the practices described below.

As the developer is based in Portugal (European Union), this policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Email address — when you create an account or sign in with Google or Apple.
  • Display name — optionally set in your profile.
  • Team follows — the sports teams you choose to follow inside the app.

Information collected automatically:

  • Push notification token — a device token generated by Apple (APNs) or Google (FCM) if you grant notification permission. Used only to deliver match alerts you have opted into.
  • Subscription status — whether you are on the Free or Pro plan, managed via RevenueCat.
  • App usage data — which matches and features you interact with, stored to personalise your experience.

Information we do NOT collect:

  • We do not collect your precise location.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, or microphone.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising profiling.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data — retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Push tokens — deleted when you revoke notification permission or delete your account.
  • Purchase records — retained as required by applicable tax and accounting law (typically 7 years in Portugal).

5. Your Rights under GDPR

As an EU resident you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction — ask us to restrict processing in certain circumstances.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw consent for push notifications at any time in your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, email solev.studio@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Portuguese data protection authority: CNPD (cnpd.pt).

6. Data Security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Authentication tokens are stored in device secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).
  • Our database uses Row-Level Security (RLS) — users can only access their own data.
  • No API keys or service credentials are included in the app binary.

7. Children's Privacy

KnowScore is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. For significant changes we will notify you via the app or by email. Continued use of KnowScore after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
