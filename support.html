Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → KnowScore → Cancel Subscription. On Android, open the Google Play Store → tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → KnowScore → Cancel.

How do I follow a team?

Go to the Following tab and tap the "+" button to search for and add any national team competing in the FIFA World Cup 2026. Free accounts can follow up to 3 teams; Premium accounts have no limit.

Why did a match prediction change?

Predictions update automatically when new information becomes available — an injury or suspension, an official lineup announcement, or a recent match result. Check the "What Changed Today" feed on the Insights tab to see the reason behind any shift.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email solev.studio@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". See our Privacy Policy for full details.

I found a bug or have a feature request.

We'd love to hear from you — email solev.studio@gmail.com with as much detail as possible (device model, iOS/Android version, and steps to reproduce if it's a bug).
